<p><ul><li>Low Frequency</li><li>High Frequency</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The textile metal detector market is segmented into low frequency and high frequency types, each serving distinct applications. Low frequency detectors, typically operating below 1 kHz, penetrate deeper and are effective in identifying larger metal objects, making them suitable for heavy-duty industrial use. In contrast, high frequency detectors, usually operating above 1 kHz, offer superior sensitivity for detecting smaller metallic items, making them ideal for fine detection in textiles and delicate materials. Both types cater to specific detection needs within the industry.</p></p>
