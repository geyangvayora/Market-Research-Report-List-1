<p><p>The digital intraoral scanner market comprises two primary types: white light scanners and laser scanners. White light scanners capture detailed 3D images of dental structures using structured light technology, offering high accuracy and speed in scanning. In contrast, laser scanners utilize laser beams to obtain precise measurements and generate detailed digital impressions. Both types enhance the efficiency of dental procedures, improve patient comfort, and facilitate better communication between dental professionals and patients, ultimately driving their adoption in modern dentistry.</p></p>
