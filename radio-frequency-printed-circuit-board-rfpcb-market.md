<p><p>The Radio Frequency Printed Circuit Board (RFPCB) market is categorized into three primary types: Metal, Ceramic, and PTFE. Metal RFPCBs, often made from aluminum or copper, offer excellent thermal conductivity and durability, making them suitable for high-frequency applications. Ceramic RFPCBs provide enhanced dielectric properties and stability across a wide range of temperatures, ideal for demanding environments. PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) RFPCBs boast low dielectric loss and high-frequency performance, making them preferable for high-speed and high-frequency electronic systems. Each type serves distinct applications based on performance requirements.</p></p>
