<p><p>The etching system in semiconductor manufacturing is crucial for defining circuit patterns on wafers. There are two primary types: dry etch systems and wet etch systems. Dry etch systems utilize gases to remove material with high precision and are ideal for intricate designs, often used in advanced nodes. In contrast, wet etch systems involve chemical solutions to etch material and are suitable for less detailed applications. Both systems play essential roles in producing reliable and efficient semiconductor components.</p></p>
