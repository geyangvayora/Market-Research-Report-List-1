<p><ul><li>Biochemical Diagnosis</li><li>Immune Diagnosis</li><li>Molecular Diagnosis</li><li>Microbiological Diagnosis</li><li>Urine Diagnosis</li><li>Coagulation Diagnosis</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IVD Infectious Disease market comprises various diagnostic types. Biochemical diagnosis involves analyzing bodily fluids for pathogens using chemical methods. Immune diagnosis focuses on detecting antibodies or antigens related to infections. Molecular diagnosis employs techniques like PCR to identify genetic material from pathogens. Microbiological diagnosis isolates and identifies microorganisms from samples. Urine diagnosis tests urine for infectious agents. Coagulation diagnosis assesses blood clotting factors related to infections. Other market segments include rapid tests and point-of-care diagnostics not classified in the main categories.</p></p>
