<p><p>The silica flour filler market comprises three main types: Crystal Quartz Powder, Fused Silica Powder, and Spherical Quartz Powder. Crystal Quartz Powder is characterized by its natural crystalline structure, making it suitable for various applications in construction and ceramics. Fused Silica Powder, produced by melting high-purity silica, offers superior thermal stability and is ideal for precision casting and electronics. Spherical Quartz Powder, with its uniform particle shape, is used extensively in advanced applications like coatings and polymer composites, enhancing performance and durability.</p></p>
