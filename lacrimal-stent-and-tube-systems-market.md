<p><strong>The Lacrimal Stent and Tube Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monocanalicular Stents</li><li>Bicanalicular Stents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lacrimal stent and tube systems market is primarily categorized into monocanalicular and bicanalicular stents. Monocanalicular stents are designed to traverse a single tear duct, facilitating drainage while minimizing intervention. In contrast, bicanalicular stents involve both tear ducts, offering a more comprehensive solution for more severe blockage conditions. Both types serve to alleviate issues related to lacrimal drainage disorders, helping to restore proper tear flow and alleviate symptoms, ultimately improving patients' quality of life.</p></p>
