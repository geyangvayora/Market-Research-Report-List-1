<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Plasma Therapy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pure PRP</li><li>Leucocyte-rich PRP</li><li>Leukocyte-rich Fibrin (L-PRF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Plasma Therapy market comprises three primary types: Pure Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP), Leucocyte-Rich PRP, and Leukocyte-Rich Fibrin (L-PRF). Pure PRP focuses on a high concentration of platelets with minimal white blood cells, promoting healing and tissue regeneration. Leucocyte-Rich PRP includes an elevated leukocyte count, enhancing immune response and inflammation control. L-PRF combines platelets and leukocytes in a fibrin matrix, allowing for sustained growth factor release, making it ideal for hard and soft tissue healing in various medical applications.</p></p>
