<p><p>The oilseed processing equipment market comprises various types that facilitate the transformation of oilseeds into usable oil and by-products. Oilseed cleaning equipment removes impurities and foreign materials. Hulling equipment separates hulls from seeds, enhancing extraction efficiency. Crushing equipment breaks down seeds for oil retrieval, while oilseed presses mechanically extract oil from seeds. Oil extraction equipment further refines the process using solvents, ensuring maximum yield. Additionally, "others" includes ancillary tools and technologies that support oilseed processing operations.</p></p>
