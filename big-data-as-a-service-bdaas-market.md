<p><p>Big Data as a Service (BDaaS) enables organizations to manage and analyze large datasets without investing in extensive infrastructure. The market is categorized into two types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise BDaaS involves deploying data management systems within an organization's infrastructure, offering greater control but requiring significant investment. In contrast, cloud-based BDaaS offers scalability and flexibility, allowing users to access data services over the internet, reducing upfront costs and maintenance while improving accessibility and collaboration.</p></p>
