<p><p>The Smart Greenhouse Irrigation System market encompasses various irrigation types designed to optimize water use. Micro sprinkler irrigation disperses water in fine droplets, ensuring even coverage over larger areas. Boom irrigation utilizes a movable arm to provide targeted water application, ideal for expansive crops.
