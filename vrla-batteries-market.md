<p><p>The VRLA (Valve Regulated Lead Acid) battery market primarily features two types: Absorbed Glass Mat (AGM) batteries and Gel batteries. AGM batteries utilize a fiberglass mat to absorb electrolyte, providing high efficiency and superior cycle life, making them ideal for high-drain applications. In contrast, Gel batteries contain a thickened electrolyte, offering deep discharge capabilities and better performance in extreme temperatures. Both types are maintenance-free and leak-proof, widely used in renewable energy systems, telecommunications, and backup power solutions.</p></p>
