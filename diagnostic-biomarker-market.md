S100 is often associated with melanoma, while MART-1 and Gp100 are specific to melanoma cell differentiation. HMB45 is used in melanoma diagnosis, and AFP is a marker for liver cancer. The "others" category includes emerging biomarkers and those used for various cancers and diseases, expanding the market's scope and utility.</p></p>
