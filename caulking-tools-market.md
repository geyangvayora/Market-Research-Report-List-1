<p><p>The caulking tools market is categorized into three main types: manual, air, and electric caulking tools. Air caulking tools utilize compressed air for a more efficient and consistent application, ideal for larger projects. Electric caulking tools offer even greater convenience and precision, using battery or corded power to dispense materials effortlessly. Each type caters to varying user needs and project scales, enhancing efficiency and ease of use.</p></p>
