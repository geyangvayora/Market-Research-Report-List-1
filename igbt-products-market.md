<p><p>The IGBT products market encompasses three primary types: IGBT Chips, IGBT Single Tubes, and IGBT Modules. IGBT Chips are the fundamental semiconductor devices, used for switching and amplification in electronic circuits. IGBT Single Tubes consist of a single IGBT device, compact and suitable for applications requiring less power. IGBT Modules combine multiple IGBT chips in a single package, delivering higher power capacity and efficiency, making them ideal for industrial applications such as inverters, motor drives, and energy systems.</p></p>
