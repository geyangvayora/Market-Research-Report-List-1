<p><strong>The Gold Mining Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Placer Mining</li><li>Sluicing</li><li>Gold Panning</li><li>Dredging</li><li>Hard-Rock Mining</li><li>Rocker Box</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gold mining encompasses various methods to extract gold from the earth. Placer mining targets loose, sediment-filled areas, while sluicing utilizes water to separate gold from gravel. Gold panning is a manual technique involving a pan to wash away lighter materials. Dredging involves removing sediment from water bodies to extract gold. Hard-rock mining focuses on extracting gold embedded in solid rock. The rocker box serves as a simple device to separate gold from soil using water flow. Each method is tailored to specific environmental conditions and gold deposits.</p></p>
