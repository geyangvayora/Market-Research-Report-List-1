<p><p>The aseptic isolators market is categorized into two main types: open isolators and closed isolators. Open isolators facilitate aseptic operations in environments where operators may have direct contact with non-sterile components. In contrast, closed isolators are designed to maintain a sterile environment, preventing any exposure to outside contaminants. These systems are crucial in pharmaceuticals and biotechnology for ensuring the integrity of sensitive products during manufacturing. The choice between them depends on the specific requirements of contamination control and product safety.</p></p>
