<p><p>The BOPP labels market encompasses various types, including pressure-sensitive labels, shrink labels, wet glue labels, and others. Pressure-sensitive labels adhere without additional adhesives, offering versatility for various applications. Shrink labels shrink to fit the product shape when heated, providing a tight seal and 360-degree branding. Wet glue labels require an adhesive applied during labeling, often used for bottles and jars. Other label types may include in-mold and laser labels, catering to specialized needs in packaging and branding across industries.</p></p>
