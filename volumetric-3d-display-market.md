<p><strong>The Volumetric 3D Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Swept-Volume Display</li><li>Solid-Volume Display</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The volumetric 3D display market comprises two primary types: swept-volume displays and solid-volume displays. Swept-volume displays create 3D visuals by moving light through a defined space, often employing rotating mechanisms or dynamic screens to generate images that appear volumetric. In contrast, solid-volume displays use a fixed medium where images are rendered within a solid volume, allowing for persistent 3D representations without the need for motion. Both technologies offer immersive visual experiences for various applications, from entertainment to medical imaging.</p></p>
