<p><ul><li>Dynamic Microphones</li><li>Condenser Microphones</li><li>Ribbon Microphones</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The overhead microphone market comprises three main types: dynamic, condenser, and ribbon microphones. Dynamic microphones are robust and ideal for high sound pressure levels, making them suitable for live performances. Condenser microphones offer superior sensitivity and frequency response, making them preferred in studio settings for capturing nuanced sounds. Ribbon microphones, known for their warm tone and natural sound reproduction, are favored in specific musical genres and professional applications. Each type caters to different audio needs in various environments.</p></p>
