<p><p>The Emission Control Catalyst for Small Engines Market is segmented into Diesel-Based and Gasoline-Based Emission Catalysts. Diesel-Based Emission Catalysts primarily focus on reducing nitrogen oxides (NOx) and particulate matter, often using selective catalytic reduction (SCR) and diesel particulate filters (DPF). In contrast, Gasoline-Based Emission Catalysts target carbon monoxide (CO), hydrocarbons (HC), and NOx, utilizing three-way catalysts that facilitate simultaneous conversion. Both types are crucial for meeting stringent environmental regulations and enhancing engine performance while reducing environmental impact.</p></p>
