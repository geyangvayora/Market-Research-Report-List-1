<p><p>The Computer-Aided Engineering (CAE) market encompasses various analytical tools, primarily Finite Element Analysis (FEA) and Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD). FEA focuses on predicting how structures respond to forces, enabling engineers to simulate physical behavior under various conditions, which is vital in fields like automotive and aerospace. In contrast, CFD analyzes fluid flow and heat transfer, essential for optimizing designs in industries such as oil and gas, HVAC, and automotive. Both tools enhance product design efficiency and performance assessment.</p></p>
