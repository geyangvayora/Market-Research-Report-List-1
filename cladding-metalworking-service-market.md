<p><p>The cladding service market in metalworking encompasses various techniques for combining materials to enhance properties. Roll bonding involves joining layers through pressure and heat, creating strong bonds ideal for manufacturing composite materials. Explosive welding uses controlled detonations to merge metals with high strength and minimal heat-affected zones, suitable for dissimilar materials. Laser cladding employs focused laser energy to melt and fuse powders onto substrates, providing precision and improved surface characteristics. Each method serves specialized applications across diverse industries.</p></p>
