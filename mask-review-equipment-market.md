<p><strong>The Mask Review Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mask Review Equipment market is crucial for ensuring the quality of semiconductor manufacturing processes. The Die to Die (DD) method involves comparing and analyzing individual semiconductor dies to detect defects, ensuring uniform quality across different chips. In contrast, the Die to Database (DB) method involves referencing a database of known good dies to identify discrepancies and potential defects in newly produced chips. Both methods enhance defect detection and improve yield rates in semiconductor production.</p></p>
