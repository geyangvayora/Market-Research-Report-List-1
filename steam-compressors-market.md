<p><ul><li>Roots-Type</li><li>Centrifugal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steam compressors market primarily features two main types: Roots-type and centrifugal compressors. Roots-type compressors use two meshing lobes to compress steam, providing high efficiency and reliability, ideal for low-pressure applications. In contrast, centrifugal compressors utilize rotational energy from a spinning impeller to achieve high-pressure steam transportation, making them suitable for large-scale operations. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, balancing factors like pressure, volume, and energy efficiency to optimize performance across various applications.</p></p>
