<p><ul><li>M-JPEG Technology</li><li>MPEG-4 Technology</li><li>H.264 Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HD Digital Video Servers market encompasses various technologies, each with distinct features. M-JPEG technology compresses video using individual JPEG images, providing high-quality visuals but requiring more bandwidth. MPEG-4 technology improves compression efficiency, enabling higher-quality video at lower bitrates, making it suitable for streaming. H.264 technology, a widely adopted codec, further enhances compression without significant quality loss, supporting HD video playback and distribution. These technologies cater to diverse applications, including broadcasting, streaming services, and video conferencing.</p></p>
