<p><ul><li>Mechanical Dampers</li><li>Intumescent Dampers</li><li>Air Transfer Fire Dampers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire damper market includes three main types: Mechanical Dampers, Intumescent Dampers, and Air Transfer Fire Dampers. Mechanical Dampers operate using a mechanical mechanism to close off airflow during a fire, preventing smoke spread. Intumescent Dampers utilize materials that expand when exposed to high temperatures, sealing off openings effectively. Air Transfer Fire Dampers allow air to pass under normal conditions but close to restrict smoke and fire spread during emergencies, ensuring safety in buildings.</p></p>
