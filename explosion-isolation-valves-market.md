<p><p>The Explosion Isolation Valves market is categorized into two main types: horizontal and vertical explosion isolation valves. Horizontal valves are designed to perform effectively in pipelines running parallel to the ground, providing robust protection against backflow pressure from explosions. In contrast, vertical valves are employed in systems where space constraints dictate an upright design, effectively isolating explosive forces in vertical pipelines. Both types are crucial in preventing the propagation of explosions in industrial environments, enhancing safety and system integrity.</p></p>
