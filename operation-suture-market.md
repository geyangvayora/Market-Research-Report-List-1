<p><p>Operation Suture involves the use of sutures in surgical procedures to close wounds and incisions. The market is categorized into two main types: absorbable sutures and non-absorbable sutures. Absorbable sutures are designed to naturally dissolve over time, eliminating the need for removal, and are often used in internal procedures. Non-absorbable sutures remain in the body unless surgically removed, making them suitable for external wounds or long-term support. Each type serves distinct medical needs, influencing purchase decisions in the healthcare sector.</p></p>
