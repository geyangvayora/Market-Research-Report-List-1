<p><p>The 3D printing resin pigment market comprises two primary types: liquid pigments and pigment powders. Liquid pigments are pre-dispersed in a liquid medium, offering easier integration into resin formulations for consistent coloring and smooth application. Conversely, pigment powders are solid granules that must be carefully blended with resin to achieve the desired color and properties. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the aesthetic appeal and functionality of 3D printed objects, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
