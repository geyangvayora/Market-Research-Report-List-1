<p><p>Pharmacy Information Systems are categorized into three market types: web-based, on-premises, and cloud-based. On-premises systems are installed locally, offering control and security but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. Each type caters to different operational needs and organizational preferences within pharmacies.</p></p>
