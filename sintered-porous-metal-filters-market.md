<p><p>Sintered porous metal filters are categorized by porosity levels, affecting their applications and performance. Low porosity filters (below 30%) offer fine filtration, suitable for capturing small particles in demanding environments. Medium porosity filters (30-60%) balance flow rate and filtration efficiency, making them suitable for various industrial processes. High porosity filters (above 60%) provide high flow rates with reduced resistance, ideal for applications where rapid fluid movement is essential, such as in gas filtration or as part of cooling systems.</p></p>
