<p><p>Electric Vehicle Supply Equipment (EVSE) encompasses two primary market types: DC charging and AC charging. DC charging provides fast charging by delivering direct current directly to the vehicle's battery, allowing for rapid replenishment, typically within 30 minutes. AC charging, on the other hand, uses alternating current, requiring the vehicle's onboard charger to convert it to direct current, resulting in longer charging times, often several hours. Both types cater to different user needs, from quick top-ups to overnight charging.</p></p>
