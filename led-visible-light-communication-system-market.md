<p><p>LED Visible Light Communication (VLC) System utilizes light-emitting diodes (LEDs) to transmit data wirelessly through visible light. This technology enables high-speed communication by modulating the intensity of light emitted, which can be decoded by photodetectors. VLC offers several advantages over traditional wireless communication methods, including greater bandwidth, reduced interference, and enhanced security, making it particularly suitable for environments where radio frequency communication may not be ideal.</p><p>The LED Visible Light Communication System Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.6% during the forecast period. <p><p>The LED Visible Light Communication (VLC) System market is primarily divided into two types: PC-LED and RGB-LED. PC-LED (Polymeric Color-LED) utilizes a single color light source, primarily for effective communication in various applications like indoor positioning and data transfer, offering higher efficiency. In contrast, RGB-LEDs comprise red, green, and blue elements, allowing for a broader color spectrum and increased data transmission capacity, making them ideal for applications requiring high-quality visuals and robust communication capabilities, such as smart lighting and advertising.</p></p>
