<p><p>The low voltage switchboards market primarily consists of single front and double front types. Single front switchboards feature all components accessible from one side, making them ideal for space-constrained installations. In contrast, double front switchboards provide access to both sides, facilitating maintenance and operation, thereby enhancing functionality in larger setups. These market segments cater to different operational needs, with single front suited for compact spaces and double front designed for more extensive systems requiring ease of access and improved safety.</p></p>
