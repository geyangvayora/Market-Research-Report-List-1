<p><ul><li>Active Extruded Aluminum Heat Sink</li><li>Passive Extruded Aluminum Heat Sink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The extruded aluminum heat sink market is categorized into active and passive types. Active extruded aluminum heat sinks employ mechanical means, like fans or pumps, to enhance heat dissipation, improving cooling efficiency in applications where heat generation is high. In contrast, passive extruded aluminum heat sinks rely on natural convection and radiation to dissipate heat without any moving parts, making them more energy-efficient and suitable for applications with lower heat output. Both types play crucial roles in thermal management across various industries.</p></p>
