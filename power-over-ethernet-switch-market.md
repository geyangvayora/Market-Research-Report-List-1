<p><p>Power over Ethernet (PoE) switches are networking devices that provide both data and electrical power to connected devices over standard Ethernet cabling. This technology simplifies the installation of networked devices, such as IP cameras, wireless access points, and VoIP phones, by eliminating the need for separate power sources. The growing adoption of smart building technologies, coupled with the increasing need for efficient energy management solutions, has significantly contributed to the expansion of the PoE switch market.</p><p>The Power Over Ethernet Switch Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.7% during the forecast period. Furthermore, the integration of PoE technology with Power over HDBaseT, enabling longer-distance power delivery, is gaining traction. Demand for 802.3bt standard-compliant devices, which support increased power delivery, is also on the rise.
