<p><p>The ultra-clear screen protectors market primarily consists of three types: PET, tempered glass, and other materials. PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) protectors are thin, flexible, and offer basic protection against scratches. Tempered glass protectors are made from treated glass, providing superior durability and impact resistance, often with added features like anti-glare or anti-fingerprint coatings. Other market types may include flexible polymer films or specialized coatings that enhance clarity, offering varied levels of protection and user experience tailored to different devices.</p></p>
