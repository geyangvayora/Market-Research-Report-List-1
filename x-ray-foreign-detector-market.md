<p><p>The X-ray foreign detector market consists primarily of two types: Digital Radiography (DR) and Computed Tomography (CT). Digital Radiography uses digital X-ray sensors to capture images, providing immediate visualization and enhancing image quality while reducing radiation exposure. In contrast, Computed Tomography employs X-ray technology to create detailed cross-sectional images of the body by rotating around the area of interest, allowing for precise diagnosis and treatment planning. Both types significantly contribute to advancing medical imaging and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
