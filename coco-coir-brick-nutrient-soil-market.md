<p><p>Coco coir brick nutrient soil is available in two main types: buffered and unbuffered coco coir. Buffered coco coir is treated to neutralize salts and enhance its nutrient-holding capacity, making it ideal for seedlings and sensitive plants. In contrast, unbuffered coco coir retains its natural state, providing a lightweight growing medium but may require additional nutrient management as it can leach essential minerals. Both types serve distinct purposes in the horticultural market, targeting various gardening needs and plant development stages.</p></p>
