<p><p>The fluidized-bed dryer market is categorized into two main types: single-layer and multi-layer systems. Single-layer fluidized-bed dryers utilize a single layer of material, facilitating efficient heat transfer and moisture removal for small to medium batches. In contrast, multi-layer fluidized-bed dryers feature multiple layers of material, allowing for higher throughput and more effective processing of larger quantities. Both types serve various industries, including food, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals, offering tailored solutions for drying needs based on operational requirements.</p></p>
