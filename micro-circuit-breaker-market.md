<p><p>The micro circuit breaker market features different types based on the number of poles. Two-pole breakers are commonly used in single-phase applications, providing protection for both the phase and neutral wires. Three-pole breakers are suitable for three-phase systems, managing the load balance across all phases. Four-pole breakers combine three-phase protection with a neutral connection, ideal for more complex systems. Each type offers specific functionality, catering to diverse electrical requirements and enhancing safety in various applications.</p></p>
