<p><p>A Vascular Compression Unit (VCU) enhances circulation and reduces swelling through intermittent pneumatic compression. The market is categorized into hot therapy and cold therapy. Hot therapy uses warmth to relax muscles and improve blood flow, aiding in recovery and pain relief. Cold therapy, on the other hand, targets inflammation and pain management by applying cold temperatures to constrict blood vessels and reduce swelling. Both types serve critical roles in post-injury rehabilitation and overall vascular health.</p></p>
