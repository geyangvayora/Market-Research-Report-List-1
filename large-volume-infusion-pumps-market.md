<p><ul><li>Fully Automatic</li><li>Semi Automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Large Volume Infusion Pumps are classified into two main types: fully automatic and semi-automatic. Fully automatic pumps operate independently, delivering precise fluid volumes based on pre-set parameters, minimizing the need for manual intervention. They enhance accuracy and efficiency in medication administration. Conversely, semi-automatic pumps require some level of manual control, allowing healthcare professionals to adjust infusion rates or volumes as needed. Both types play a crucial role in patient care, particularly in critical and surgical settings, ensuring safe and effective fluid management.</p></p>
