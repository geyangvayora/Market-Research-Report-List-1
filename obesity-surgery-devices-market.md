<p><p>The obesity surgery devices market encompasses various types aimed at treating severe obesity. Intragastric balloons are non-surgical devices that occupy space in the stomach, promoting early satiety. Gastric electrical stimulation involves implanting a device that sends electrical signals to the stomach, regulating appetite. Gastric bands are adjustable devices placed around the upper stomach to create a smaller pouch, while staples are used in bariatric surgery to permanently reduce stomach size. Each method offers unique benefits for weight management.</p></p>
