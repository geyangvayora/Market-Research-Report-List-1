<p><ul><li>CMC Matrix Converter</li><li>IMC Matrix Converter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The matrix converter market is primarily divided into two types: the CMC (Conventional Matrix Converter) and the IMC (Indirect Matrix Converter). CMCs directly convert AC power from one frequency to another without intermediate DC conversion, offering high efficiency and compact design for applications like variable speed drives. IMCs, on the other hand, utilize an intermediate DC link, allowing for enhanced control and flexibility in power conversion. Both types are increasingly adopted in renewable energy and electric vehicle sectors for their advanced performance characteristics.</p></p>
