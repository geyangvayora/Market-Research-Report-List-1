<p><strong>The Pressure Transmitters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absolute Pressure Transmitters</li><li>Differential Pressure Transmitters</li><li>Gauge Pressure Transmitters</li><li>Multivariable Transmitters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pressure transmitters are devices used to measure pressure in various applications. Absolute pressure transmitters measure pressure relative to a perfect vacuum, making them ideal for scientific applications. Differential pressure transmitters measure the difference between two pressure points, commonly used in flow measurement and filtration. Gauge pressure transmitters assess pressure relative to atmospheric pressure, suitable for various industrial uses. Multivariable transmitters deliver multiple measurements (pressure, temperature, flow) from one device, enhancing efficiency and reducing installation costs in complex systems.</p></p>
