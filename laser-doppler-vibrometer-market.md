<p><p>The Laser Doppler Vibrometer market comprises three main types: single-point vibrometers, scanning vibrometers, and others. Single-point vibrometers are designed for precise vibration measurements at specific points, suitable for static or low-frequency applications. Scanning vibrometers can cover larger areas, enabling the analysis of vibration patterns over surfaces, ideal for dynamic studies. The "others" category includes specialized or hybrid devices that combine features of both types or serve niche applications, expanding the versatility of vibration measurement techniques.</p></p>
