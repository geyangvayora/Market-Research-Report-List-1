<p><p>Pipe extrusion lines are essential for manufacturing plastic pipes, and they come in two primary types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical pipe extrusion lines utilize a vertical setup, facilitating gravity-driven processes and enabling efficient material flow, often used for smaller pipe diameters. In contrast, horizontal extrusion lines are designed for larger outputs and greater flexibility, supporting various pipe sizes with enhanced cooling efficiency. Both types play crucial roles in different market segments, catering to specific production needs and operational efficiencies.</p></p>
