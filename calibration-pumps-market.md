<p><p>Calibration pumps are essential tools used for testing and calibrating pressure measurement instruments. Hydraulic calibration pumps utilize water or oil to generate high pressure, making them suitable for testing devices requiring high accuracy in harsh conditions. In contrast, pneumatic calibration pumps employ compressed air to create pressure, offering portability and ease of use for lower pressure applications. Both types serve critical roles in various industries, ensuring the precision and reliability of pressure measurements across different environments and applications.</p></p>
