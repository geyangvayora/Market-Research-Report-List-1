<p><p>The laser scanners market is categorized into three types based on their range capabilities. Long-range laser scanners are designed for extensive surveying applications, capturing data from distances of over 300 meters, making them ideal for large-scale projects like infrastructure or landscape mapping. Intermediate-range laser scanners cover distances between 30 to 300 meters, suitable for urban modeling and construction monitoring. Short-range laser scanners operate within a few centimeters to 30 meters, typically used for detailed scanning of structures and intricate features in confined spaces.</p></p>
