<p><ul><li>Forward-Curved Air Blowers</li><li>Backward-Inclined and Backward-Curved Air Blowers</li><li>Radial Air Blowers</li><li>Airfoil Air Blowers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air blower market includes various types tailored for specific applications. Forward-curved air blowers are efficient for low-pressure, high-capacity airflow, ideal for ventilation. Backward-inclined and backward-curved blowers provide higher efficiency at moderate pressures, suitable for industrial processes. Radial air blowers deliver high-pressure airflow with compact designs, commonly used in pneumatic conveying. Airfoil air blowers offer improved energy efficiency and quieter operation, making them popular in HVAC systems. Each type serves distinct operational needs across industries.</p></p>
