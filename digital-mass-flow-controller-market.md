<p><strong>The Digital Mass Flow Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Pressure Type</li><li>Differential Pressure Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Digital Mass Flow Controller (DMFC) market can be categorized into two main types: Thermal Pressure Type and Differential Pressure Type. Thermal Pressure Type DMFCs measure flow rates by assessing temperature changes caused by fluid dynamics, making them ideal for gases. Conversely, Differential Pressure Type DMFCs determine flow rates by measuring the pressure drop across a flow path, typically suited for liquids and dense gases. Both types ensure precise control and measurement in a variety of industrial applications, enhancing efficiency and performance.</p></p>
